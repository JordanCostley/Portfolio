Hello there!

I am a User Experience Designer currently at Kennesaw State University studying Interactive Design with a minor in Technical Communication. I love being able to solve problems by bridging the gap between user goals and business goals. I am continually seeking to grow and learn by interacting with the world and connecting with those around me to better myself and my work.

My time at Kennesaw State University has allowed me to explore a variety of topics and skills ranging from user interface design to usability testing. My background in visual design has allowed me to sharpen my typography and layout skills to create digital interfaces with a focus on user goals. Utilizing established design processes, I have explored design utilizing research, modeling, wireframing, and prototyping.

Having just completed my college career I have learned a variety of skills and knowledge with a foundation in human-centered design. I have a strong technical background as well as an ever-growing knowledge of theories about design and humans. I am very passionate about beautiful design that offers an efficient and seamless experience that meets user goals and bridge the gap with business goals.

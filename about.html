Hello there!

I am an undergraduate student at Kennesaw State University studying Interactive Design with a minor in Technical Communications. Ever since getting my hands on a computer I have fallen in love with learning new technologies and tools. Being in a world that is ever changing it is important to learn and adapt to a variety of situations. Having grown up in a world filled with electronics and technology I enjoy constantly learning new skills and knowledge.

I am nearing the end of my college career having learned a variety of skills and knowledge with a foundation in human centered design. I have a strong technical background as well as an ever-growing knowledge of theories about design and humans. I am very passionate about beautiful design that offers an efficient and seamless experience. I believe strongly in communicating with those around me whether that be coworkers or strangers to get a better sense of how we interact with the world.

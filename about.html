Hello there!

I am User Experience Designer currently at Kennesaw State University studying Interactive Design with a minor in Technical Communication. I love being able to solve problems by bridging the gap between user goals and business goals. I am continually seeking to grow and learn by interacting with the world and those around me to better myself and my work.

Ever since getting my hands on a computer I have fallen in love with learning new technologies and tools. Being in a world that is ever changing it is important to learn and adapt to a variety of situations. Having grown up in a world filled with technology I enjoy constantly learning new skills and knowledge. Whether this be learning photography skills or flag making I enjoy dipping my toes into a variety of things.

I am nearing the end of my college career having learned a variety of skills and knowledge with a foundation in human centered design. I have a strong technical background as well as an ever-growing knowledge of theories about design and humans. I am very passionate about beautiful design that offers an efficient and seamless experience. Having transitioned from Graphic Design to Interactive Design I have formed a strong foundation in visual design skills to pair with my love of constructing products for users based on their goals.

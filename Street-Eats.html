Overview

Street Eats is an all-encompassing food truck app. With a primary focus on locating ever relocating food trucks Street Eats aims to connect an ever-growing market of food truck entrepreneurs with those looking for new unique dining options.

Goals

1. Create an efficient means of connecting users with food truck options 2. Convey menus and reviews in an easy to digest manner 3. Allow users to discover new food venues and events

Methodology

Participants were recruited at random on the Kennesaw State Marietta campus. The testing team went around the area near where the vehicle was parked for the test and spoke with students to recruit them for participation.

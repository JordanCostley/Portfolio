design.


Here is a collection of my hand-crafted
branding and other deisng projects.

Hollydale Church

Branding and collateral for Hollydale Church based in Marietta, GA. Bringing a modern touch while holding true to an iconic mark. Collateral for a sermon series to be placed on various social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram.

Taco Grande Restaurant

Restaurant branding and menu design for a Mexican themed eatery. The design captures the energy associated with the more casual dining experience tying in with the atypical menu shape to bring life into an often cliche market.

Voyager Apparel Co. Clothing

Clothing brand branding concept for an outdoors and adventure oriented clothing company. Designed to capture the adventerous audience who have an eager desire to travel the world and find new places to fall in love with while dresssed in style.

Dare! Youth Event

A youth event aimed at encouraging the younger generations to go out and seek greater things, to dare to push themselves towards their goals. The overall design is aimed to grab the attention and interest the youth while dealing with subject matter that might often be met with apathy.

West Hill Primary School

The mark created is aimed to capture the energy and life that can be found within the lives of children while tying in with the positive effects an early start on education can have on children as they develop. The newsletter both informs the parents and students as to what they are to learn while bringing in the character of the enjoyment that is to be had with learning to interest the children.

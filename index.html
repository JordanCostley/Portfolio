Explore Asheville

Swimming in Waterfalls

Asheville is known for a variety of waterfalls, many great for swimming. There are multiple falls that offer both short hikes and longer hikes depending on one's physical abilities. Also, there are sites are both free and those with fees, be sure to check ahead before heading out on your advneture.

Hiking the Blue Ridge Mountains

Along with waterfalls, Asheville is known for being home to a large stretch of the Blue Ridge Mountains with the Blue Ridge Parkway running through a large portion of the city. Asheville offers both long and short hikes for all ages. Alternatively, you can check out the various overlooks on the parkway to save some energy and get a more relaxing experience. There are plenty of guides out on the internet that will offer information on the various trails in the area. Make sure to read up on road closures during seasons such as winter before making the drive out.

Asheville is located in western North Carolina's Blue Ridge Mountains.

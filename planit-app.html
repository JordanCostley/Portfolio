Project: Planit Mobile Concept and Prototype

Role: Researcher and Designer

Duration: In Progress | January 2020

Overview

Planit is a student planner we are currently constructing using the Goal Directed Design Process. We are creating an app that aims to benefit our peers via conducting research to discover user goals and ultimately deliver a prototype based on our findings.

Challenges
  1. Help students achieve their academic goals
  2. Encourage and simplify the process of staying organized
  3. Reduce stress of staying on track
The Team

Iegor Gutyria: Designer

Derek Duty: Designer

Progress

The project is currently under construction. We are working through the Goal Directed Design Process to ensure a product that satisfies our users' goals. The project will be completed around late April and will be updated at completion to share the full scope of the process.
